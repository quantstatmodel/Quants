                                   \( = 2\int\limits_{0}^{t} \int\limits_{u}^{t} W_u ds dW_u + \frac{t^2}{2} = 2\int\limits_{0}^{t} W_u \big( \int\limits_{u}^{t} ds \big) dW_u + \frac{t^2}{2} = 2\int\limits_{0}^{t} \big( t-u \big) W_u dW_u + \frac{t^2}{2} \)
            For insight on Integrations' exchange see Link.

So, \( \mathbb{COV} \left[ \int\limits_{0}^{t} {W_s}^3 dW_s, \int\limits_{0}^{t} {W_s}^2 ds \right] = \mathbb{E} \left[ \big( \int\limits_{0}^{t} {W_s}^3 dW_s \big)\big( 2\int\limits_{0}^{t} \big( t-s \big) W_s dW_s + \frac{t^2}{2} \big) \right] \)

This gives :
\( 2 \mathbb{E} \left[ \big( \int\limits_{0}^{t} {W_s}^3 dW_s \big)\big( \int\limits_{0}^{t} \big( t-s \big) W_s dW_s \big) \right] + 0 = 2 \mathbb{E} \left[ \int\limits_{0}^{t} {W_s}^3 \big( t-s \big) W_s ds \right] = 2 \int\limits_{0}^{t} \big( t-s \big) \mathbb{E} \left[ {W_s}^4 \right] ds \)

From above Expressions, We can easily calculate final Expression for the Variance.
